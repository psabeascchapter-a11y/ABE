Mission and Vision

Mission

The Department of Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering is committed to producing internationally competitive engineers through excellence in instruction, research, and extension services.

Vision

A premier engineering department cultivating skilled and globally competitive professionals who promote sustainable agriculture and biosystems innovation.

Welcome Message from the Program Chair

Welcome to the Department of Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering. Our department aims to develop competent engineers equipped with knowledge in engineering, agriculture, and sustainable biosystems technologies.

Through academic excellence and strong collaboration, we prepare students to become leaders who will contribute to the modernization of agriculture and rural development.
